AYURVEDA

What is Ayurveda?

Ayurveda is an ancient system of natural medicine that originated in India over 3,000 years ago. The word Ayurveda means "the knowledge of life," combining the Sanskrit words "Ayur" (life) and "Veda" (knowledge). It focuses on maintaining balance in the body, mind, and spirit to promote good health and prevent illness. Ayurveda uses natural methods such as herbal remedies, a balanced diet, yoga, meditation, and daily routines. It believes that every person has a unique body type made up of three energies called doshas ‚Äî Vata, Pitta, and Kapha ‚Äî and that health can be achieved by keeping these in balance.
